Commands & Permissions

All available commands and their required permissions.

Commands

CommandAliasDescriptionPermission
/thebrewingmarket /tbm Opens the sell GUI thebrewingmarket.use
/thebrewingmarket reload /tbm reload Reloads configuration thebrewingmarket.admin
/thebrewingmarket history <player> <time> [page] /tbm history View sell history thebrewingmarket.history

History Arguments

ArgumentStatusDescription
<player>RequiredTarget player name
<time>RequiredTime range: {number}{unit}
[page]OptionalPage number (default: 1)

Time Units

UnitMeaningExample
sSeconds30s
mMinutes15m
hHours6h
dDays7d
wWeeks2w
MMonths (30d)3M
YYears (365d)1Y
Example: /tbm history Steve 7d 2 — page 2 of Steve's last 7 days.
