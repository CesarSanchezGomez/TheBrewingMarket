TheBrewingMarket

Sell your TheBrewingProject or BreweryX brews through a customizable GUI market with Vault economy integration.

Get Started Commands

Features

Customizable GUI

Symbol-based layout system. Every slot, icon, and decoration is configurable.

Dynamic Pricing

Price = base_price × quality_score. Configure per recipe.

Sell & Sell All

Sell items in the GUI, or scan the entire inventory at once.

Database Support

Sell history with SQLite, MySQL, or MariaDB.

Shulker Support

Sell brews inside shulker boxes. Permission-controlled.

Multi-language

MiniMessage formatting. Ships with English and Spanish.

PlaceholderAPI

28+ placeholders for scoreboards, holograms, and condition plugins.

Quick Start

Install dependencies

Paper 1.21.9+, Vault, and TheBrewingProject or BreweryX (at least one).

Drop the JAR

Place TheBrewingMarket.jar in your plugins/ folder and restart.

Configure

Edit files in plugins/TheBrewingMarket/. Use /tbm reload to apply.
